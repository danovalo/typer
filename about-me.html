What can I tell you about me?

A native of Ottawa, Nigel is a new comer to the business world but a veteran to the car world. Nigel has spent much of his youth and teenage years buying and modifying cars. Often attending many local shows, Nigel has had his personal car featured in the Ottawa Gatineau International Car Show (2018), Hondafest Canada, Meat & Grease Ottawa, Upperclass Showcase Ottawa, and is often featured on Honda Canada’s social media accounts. Cars have been a big part of his life since he can remember, and as co-founder of Garage Queen, he’s taking that passion and bringing his knowledge of the industry directly to you.

After acquiring an Honours degree in Criminology and working in his field, Nigel recognizes how difficult it can be to manage your personal vehicle while working a Monday to Friday 9 to 5. Compounding this is the added responsibility of preparing your vehicle for the harsh Canadian winter, which can often arrive unexpectedly and never with any consideration of your schedule. At Garage Queen, we handle the management of your vehicle so you can handle the management of your life.

With Nigel you can expect to deal with an eager, young professional that is as passionate about your car, as he is his own. When Nigel isn’t hands on with Garage Queen, you can catch him around local events in his 2017 Honda Civic Type-R or spending time at his local gym. On his off days, Nigel loves to spend time with his family and cat Chester.

Nigel is always excited to connect, you can find him on Instagram at @jusmytyp.r – send him a message!
